## 🛠️ QA & Reliability Roadmap (Step-by-Step)
I manage the final check-off process before any service is delivered to our clients:

### **Step 1: Automated Script Validation**
- **Process:** Stress-testing our internal contribution-push tools to ensure they operate within safe rate limits.
- **Goal:** To prevent any account flags and ensure 100% "Green Dot" stability.

### **Step 2: Profile Integrity Audits**
- **Process:** Reviewing the social proof delivery (Stars, Forks, Followers) for consistency and retention.
- **Goal:** Identifying and removing any low-quality engagement to maintain a premium profile look.

### **Step 3: Security & Compliance Testing**
- **Process:** Verifying MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) and proxy-consistency for verified digital assets.
- **Goal:** Ensuring every account handed over is secure, hardened, and ready for long-term use.

### **Step 4: Continuous Monitoring**
- **Process:** 24/7 monitoring of delivered projects to ensure long-term retention of stats.
- **Goal:** Guaranteeing the "GMBSells Quality Seal" for every transaction.
